Frog Board · Reminder Loop

Offload the
nagging.

A coordinator's real job isn't the spreadsheet, it's chasing people. The reminder loop does the chasing, in the festival's own voice, so nobody gets a guilt text and every gap heals itself.

一

A ladder, not a loop

Three touches, three jobs: confirm a week out, prepare two nights before, nudge the morning of. Each email earns its place. None repeats.

二

One tap to keep or release

Every reminder carries I'll be there and I can't make it. A decline reopens the slot on the board and pings the organizer. The nag becomes self-healing coverage.

三

The right inbox, no logins for anyone

Older scouts get reminded themselves; for the youngest, it goes to a parent, addressed about their kid. Either way the last name stays private and a tap is identity. No app, no password.

Prototype, not wired in. It rides the existing pieces: captured email, device claimToken, and the schedule (date / startAt / dueBy). The build adds Resend, a scheduler, and an unsubscribe. Tap the buttons in the phone to walk the loop.
