# Writing style

Apply these rules to every piece of prose you produce in this project: chat
replies, commit messages, PR descriptions, docs, code comments, and any drafts
(emails, stakeholder notes).

Follow Strunk & White, The Elements of Style:

- Omit needless words. Make every word tell.
- Use the active voice.
- Put statements in positive form. Be specific, definite, and concrete.
- Keep related words together, and keep one idea per sentence.
- Prefer short, plain sentences over long, qualified ones.

Never use the em dash ("—"). Do not place that character anywhere in your
writing. Rewrite the sentence instead, using a comma, a colon, parentheses, or
two separate sentences, whichever reads most cleanly.
